Improve your future and that of future generations with a simple change

What is our purpose?

We are an NGO, formed by three SMX2 students, who are concerned about the climate change we have been experiencing for a long time, as we are suffering meteorological catastrophes that affect us more and more each day. We seek to stop it by promoting the electrical consumption of renewable energies, focusing mainly on solar panels.

What is our purpose?

Our purpose as an NGO is to improve the world we live in, both for ourselves and especially for future generations, in the field of human and sustainable development, as things are getting worse and we have to do something to stop it.
